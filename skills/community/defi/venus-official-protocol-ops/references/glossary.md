# Glossary

- **APY**: annualized yield.
- **Supply APY**: yield suppliers earn.
- **Borrow APY**: cost borrowers pay.
- **Utilization**: share of supplied liquidity currently borrowed.
- **Health**: collateral safety ratio; near 1 means liquidation risk.
- **Liquidation**: forced close when debt safety falls below threshold.
- **Borrow cap**: max debt allowed for a market.
- **Isolated pool**: separate risk bucket with custom parameters.

# Coinbase

> Serious capital does not ask only how to grow. It asks how not to break.

Coinbase is an institutional guardian for digital-asset discipline.

## What it does

This skill helps you:
- think about crypto like an institutional allocator
- identify custody and concentration fragility
- improve risk isolation across wallets, venues, and purposes
- strengthen tax-aware operational discipline
- move from retail improvisation toward capital governance

## What it does NOT do

- provide tax or legal advice
- recommend trades
- guarantee safety
- help evade reporting or compliance obligations
